## ⚠️ ГЛАВНЫЕ РИСКИ И КАК Я СПРАВЛЯЮСЬ

### Risk 1: LEGAL — Copyright & Model Releases

```
РИСК: Кто-то загрузит чужие фото или фото без согласия
IMPACT: Судебные преследования, репутационный вред

MITIGATION:
├─ Require signed model releases (templates provided)
├─ Smart contract validates: can't upload without release hash
├─ Legal insurance ($50K/year)
├─ KYC for photographers (know your customer)
├─ DMCA takedown process (fast response)
└─ Legal team on retainer ($10K/month)

PROBABILITY: Low (1-2%) if mitigated well
```

### Risk 2: TECHNICAL — zkML Doesn't Work at Scale

```
РИСК: zkML proof generation слишком медленная или дорогая
IMPACT: Can't verify datasets efficiently

MITIGATION:
├─ Use proven EZKL library (already battle-tested)
├─ Fallback to traditional AI verification (CV model)
├─ Parallel processing (GPU acceleration)
├─ Batch processing (verify 1000s at once)
├─ Community audit (open source)
└─ Pivot to simpler metadata verification

PROBABILITY: Medium (20%) but MANAGEABLE
```

### Risk 3: MARKET — Not Enough Demand for Photo Datasets

```
РИСК: Компании предпочитают synthetic data (дешевле)
IMPACT: Low transaction volume

MITIGATION:
├─ POC with enterprise (OpenAI, Google) first
├─ Start with high-quality niche (portraits for face recognition)
├─ Emphasize privacy + ethical sourcing (advantage vs synthetic)
├─ Diversify: video, 3D, point clouds
├─ Partner with AI companies for co-development
└─ Emphasize quality over quantity

PROBABILITY: Low-Med (15%) but MANAGEABLE
```

### Risk 4: COMPETITION — Big Players Copy Us

```
РИСК: Meta/Google создадут свой децентрализованный маркетплейс
IMPACT: Pressure on pricing and adoption

MITIGATION:
├─ First-mover advantage (2-3 year lead)
├─ Community ownership (harder to copy, DAO governance)
├─ Network effects (more photos = more valuable)
├─ Specialization (we focus, they generalize)
├─ Partnership (work WITH them, not against)
└─ Technical moat (zkML + Arweave not trivial)

PROBABILITY: High (50-70%) but POSITIVE
(If Meta/Google adopt our platform = massive validation!)
```

### Risk 5: REGULATORY — Blockchain Issues

```
РИСК: Регуляторы запретят / ограничат blockchain платежи
IMPACT: Need to move to traditional rails

MITIGATION:
├─ Use established stablecoins (USDC, USDT)
├─ KYC/AML compliance (from day 1)
├─ Work with law firm on crypto compliance
├─ Multi-jurisdictional strategy
└─ Fallback to traditional payments (PayPal, Stripe)

PROBABILITY: Medium (25%) but MANAGEABLE
```
